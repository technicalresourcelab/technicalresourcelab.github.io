About the Philippine Technical Resource Lab (TRL)

Mission

TRL accelerates Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) adoption in the Philippines by fostering a developer ecosystem that connects government, industry, academia, and technology communities.

Vision

TRL envisions a thriving, open, and collaborative developer ecosystem that drives efficient, inclusive, safe, and equitable digital transformation in public services.
